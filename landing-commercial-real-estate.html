Navigating the World of Commercial Real Estate

Commercial Real Estate (CRE) encompasses a wide range of property types used for business purposes. Investing in or leasing commercial properties requires specialized knowledge and a strategic approach.

Types of Commercial Properties

  • Office Buildings: From skyscrapers to suburban office parks.
  • Retail Spaces: Shopping centers, standalone stores, malls.
  • Industrial Properties: Warehouses, distribution centers, manufacturing facilities.
  • Multifamily Housing: Apartment buildings (often considered commercial if over a certain number of units).
  • Special Purpose: Hotels, healthcare facilities, self-storage, etc.
